OMEPRAZOLE SODIUM
OME-M
40 mg Powder for Injection (I.V.) + 10 mL Diluent


"PROTON PUMP INHIBITOR"


INTRODUCTION:
  • Irreversible inhibitors of the gastric H+,K+-ATPase
  • Most potent suppressors of gastric acid secretion
  • Prodrugs that require activation in an acid environment
  • All proton pump inhibitors have equivalent efficacy at comparable doses*
USED FOR TREATING:
  • Peptic Ulcer Disease (PUD)
  • Duodenal Ulcer
  • Acute Gastritis
  • Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD)
  • Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome (ZES)
